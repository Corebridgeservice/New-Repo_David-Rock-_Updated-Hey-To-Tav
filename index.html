How you’re made, and what you’re made for.

You found your way here, and I am glad you did. Most people arrive after reading something I wrote, though it does not matter how you came. However you got here, the door is the same: a place to see yourself the way God sees you, and to begin to live like it is true.

The through-line

Four questions, asked in order

Everything I build and write serves one conviction. You were made on purpose, with purpose, and the way you are wired is not an accident to be managed. It is a design to be discovered. The work asks four questions, and the order is part of the gift.

The first question

How You’re Made

Who are you, underneath the roles and the noise? This is where everything begins, because you cannot build on a self you have never honestly seen.

The second question

What You’re Built For

What was all of that design given to you to do? The dreams you carry are not decoration. They are direction.

The third question

What You Stand On

When life leans on you, what holds? Your convictions, named clearly enough to build a family, a business, a life on them.

The fourth question

How You Walk It Out

Knowing who you are is not the same as living it. This is where the seeing becomes steps, walked out one week at a time.

Walk through the four questions

I am a husband, a father, and a builder of people, systems, and meaning. I have spent most of my life studying how people are made: what wakes them up, what holds them back, and what happens when someone finally sees their own design clearly.

Something multiplies when you work with your design instead of against it. The same hours carry further. The same gifts reach more people. And the good of it does not stop with you. It moves out toward the people you love, and past them.

The open door

Begin with the first question

How You’re Made is the door you can actually walk through. It opens with the Guide to Self. You answer a handful of questions in your own words and your own time, with no clock running and no right answers. Then a guide comes back to you, written for you alone, a mirror held up to what your answers quietly reveal about the way God made you. Three sessions, and each one sees you more deeply than the last.
